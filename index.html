About Me



I have been a software tester for 8 years. I was happy finding bugs and making software better but there seemed to be something missing. So I decided to learn to code and start making the software that I so enjoyed breaking. I have enjoyed tinkering with computers every since I was in high school. It was just so fun to break and fix things.

In my spare time I love to dream up new recipes, cook and bake. I also enjoy fermenting things but I can't seem to grow any plants. I have a natural ability to help people with their health and am usually the first peson they call when weird things are happening. I also love the mountains and hiking.
